Benefits of Biomass Pellets

  • Renewable source of energy
  • Carbon-neutral when sustainably sourced
  • Reduces waste by using agricultural residues
  • High energy density compared to raw biomass
  • Easier to transport and store

Types of Biomass Pellets

Wood Pellets

Made from compressed sawdust and wood waste, perfect for heating.

Agricultural Residue

Created from crop residues like corn stalks and rice husks.

Straw Pellets

Produced from compressed wheat, barley, or rice straw.

Grass Pellets

Made from various grasses, often used for animal bedding and heating.
